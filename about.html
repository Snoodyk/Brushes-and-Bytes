About myself:

Hello, My name is Ava Greenfield.
I am freelance creative artist and coder by day and artist by night.
After graduating my Master in contemporary Art at Paris of Art school in 2001,
I have set up my own brand Brashes and Bytes website.
My customers are mostly from Ireland, UK, USA, EU.
